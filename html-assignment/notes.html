Some information about HTML tags

!doctype HTML
is the first thing we add to our HTML document which defines what is the type of this document.
head and /head
Is the parent element
nav and /nav
the navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website
main and /main
primary content of the page
article and /article
is the tag used for containing independent piece of content. It could be shared as stand alone piece of content and will still make sense.
